# Product Vision Document

Version: 0.1
Status: Draft

## Product name

ChatBotWeb / Yoranix AI Platform

## Category

Multi-tenant AI knowledge platform for RAG chatbots and AI assistants.

## Vision statement

Enable any organisation to create secure AI assistants in minutes instead of months, without needing AI engineering expertise.

## Mission

Reduce the cost and complexity of AI adoption by providing a reusable platform for knowledge ingestion, retrieval, chatbot deployment, analytics, and future workflow automation.

## Product thesis

Most organisations do not need a fully custom chatbot. They need a reliable way to keep knowledge up to date and expose that knowledge through AI-powered interfaces.

The platform value is the operational layer around the chatbot:

- Knowledge management
- Tenant isolation
- Document lifecycle
- Approval workflows
- Retrieval quality
- Source citations
- Analytics
- Integrations
- Governance
- Cost control

## Target customers

Primary customers:

- Colleges
- Training providers
- Education consultancies
- Migration agencies
- Professional service firms
- Healthcare service providers

Secondary customers:

- Hospitality groups
- Non-profits
- Internal business teams
- Local service organisations

## Core user personas

### Super admin

A platform operator who manages clients, usage, settings, and operational health.

### Client admin

A client staff member who manages knowledge, branding, users, chatbot settings, and analytics.

### Knowledge contributor

A client staff member who uploads or edits knowledge for review.

### End user

A visitor who asks questions through the chatbot.

## Product pillars

### 1. Knowledge platform

Upload, edit, approve, expire, and manage knowledge sources.

### 2. AI platform

Retrieval, answer generation, citations, confidence checks, prompt management, and evaluation.

### 3. Deployment platform

Website widget first, then future channels such as Teams, WhatsApp, Slack, email, and voice.

### 4. Analytics platform

Questions, failed answers, missing knowledge, usage, and serving cost.

### 5. Automation platform

Future agents that can book appointments, create tickets, send forms, and update business systems.

## Product principles

1. Multi-tenant first.
2. Security before features.
3. Source-grounded answers only.
4. Clients manage their own knowledge.
5. Every workflow reduces repeated implementation work.
6. The platform stays modular for future channels and integrations.

## MVP promise

A client can log in, upload knowledge, configure branding, embed a chatbot, receive source-grounded answers, and review chat history.

## Long-term direction

The platform should evolve from chatbot platform to AI operations layer with document sync, workflow tools, human handover, integrations, reseller mode, and industry templates.
