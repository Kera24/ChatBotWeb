## Product thesis

Most organisations do not need a fully custom chatbot. They need a reliable operational platform for keeping knowledge up to date and exposing that knowledge through AI-powered interfaces.

## Target customers

- Colleges
- Training providers
- Education consultancies
- Migration agencies
- Professional service firms
- Healthcare service providers
- Hospitality businesses
- Internal business teams

## Core personas

- Super admin: platform operator
- Client admin: manages knowledge, users, branding, chatbot settings, and analytics
- Knowledge contributor: uploads or edits knowledge
- End user: asks questions through the chatbot

## Product principles

- Multi-tenant first.
- Security before features.
- Source-grounded answers only.
- Clients manage their own knowledge.
- Every workflow reduces repeated implementation work.
- The platform remains modular for future channels and integrations.

## MVP promise

A client can log in, upload knowledge, configure branding, embed a chatbot, receive source-grounded answers, and review chat history.

## MVP exclusions

Do not implement billing, full SSO, SharePoint sync, Google Drive sync, WhatsApp, Teams, voice, marketplace, fine-tuning, or advanced autonomous agents unless a new approved task exists.
